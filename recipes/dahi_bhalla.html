Dahi Bhalla

Ingredients:

  • Split & husked (or dhuli) Urad Dal
  • Chana Dal (split gram lentils)
  • Flavor enhancers
  • Baking powder
  • Yogurt
  • Spices

Steps:

  1. Soak the dal
  2. Make the Bhalla batter
  3. Whisk the batter
  4. Fry the Bhallas
  5. Soak the bhallas
  6. Remove water
  7. Prepare yogurt
  8. Assemble
